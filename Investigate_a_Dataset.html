Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

A person makes a doctor appointment, receives all the instructions and doesn't show up. which data can we relate it to?

Question(s) for Analysis¶

1-Does the age of the patient affect their showing up rate?
2-does the neighbrhood affects showing up?
3-do people with a handicap show up more?
4-do people who recieve sms notification come more often?
5-does the difference between appointment and schedule matters?
6-does the scholarship affect showing up?

We're gonna be looking into each question and explore other things that might show up along the way, and dismiss some of the possibilities

#we're gonna make a function which we are going to use multiple times in this project 
#this function takes a column name and groups it with it's show or no show counterpart 
#then divides their sizes giving us the ratio 
def showratio(columnname):
    x=df[df.noshow=='No'].groupby(['{}'.format(columnname)])
    y=df.groupby(['{}'.format(columnname)])
    return x.size()/y.size()
    
    
def noshowratio(columnname):
    x=df[df.noshow=='Yes'].groupby(['{}'.format(columnname)])
    y=df.groupby(['{}'.format(columnname)])
    return x.size()/y.size()

-for some strange reason people who recieve text messages of their appointment show up less?
